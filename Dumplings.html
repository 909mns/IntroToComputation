-Dumplings + Quick Dip-

Dumplings?

Ingridients

  • frozen dumplings
  • soy sauce
  • vinegar
  • chili flakes
  • oil

Steps

  1. Add a little oil, place dumplings flat-side down.
  2. Add a splash of water, cover, steam 5-7 min.
  3. Open lid and crisp the bottom.
  4. Mix dip and eat.

Tip?

Dish count matters. I mix the dip in the same bowl I'll eat from!
