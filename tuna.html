Tuna Mayo Rice?

Ingridients

  • rice
  • canned tuna
  • mayo
  • soy sauce or salt
  • sesame oil

Steps

  1. Heat rice.
  2. Add tuna + mayo.
  3. Add a little soy sauce and mix.
  4. Top with sesame oil if you have it.

Tip?

if it tasts boring, add one strong thing: any spicy sauce!
