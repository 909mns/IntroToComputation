Quesadilla?

Ingridients

  • tortilla
  • cheese
  • leftover meat
  • onions
  • any sause

Steps

  1. Put tortilla in a pan, add cheese.
  2. Add any leftover filling.
  3. Fold, cook until crisp, flip once.
  4. Cut and eat.

Tip?

If I'm too tired to wash a cutting board, I use kitchen scissors to cut it in the pan!
