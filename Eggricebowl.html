Egg Rice Bowl?

Ingridients

  • cooked rice
  • 1-2 eggs
  • soy sauce
  • butter or oil (optional)
  • green onion

Steps

  1. Heat rice in a pan.
  2. Scramble eggs in the same pan.
  3. Add rice back, splash soy sauce, mix.
  4. Top with whatever you have.

Tip?

No pan energy? Microwave rice + crack an egg + microwave again in short bursts!
